Answered Simply

Most health experts recommend a pH between 8.0 and 9.5 for daily drinking. Alkonic machines deliver precisely adjustable pH, letting you choose the level that suits your needs.

Water passes through platinum-coated titanium electrode plates during electrolysis. This separates it into alkaline and acidic streams, enriching the alkaline side with minerals and antioxidants.

Alkonic machines feature automatic self-cleaning. The only user action required is replacing the pre-filter every 6–12 months depending on usage. We handle everything else under our service plan.

Unique Properties of
Ionized Water

9.5 pH

Alkonic ionizers produce water with a pH of 8.5–9.5, helping neutralize excess acidity in the body and restore optimal biochemical balance.

Anti-Oxidant
-ORP

With a negative ORP (Oxidation Reduction Potential), ionized water acts as a powerful antioxidant — directly neutralizing harmful free radicals in the body.

Electrolysis restructures water molecules into smaller clusters (5–6 molecules vs. 12–16 in regular water), enabling faster cellular absorption and hydration.

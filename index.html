FAQs

Common Questions

What is alkaline ionized water and how does it differ from regular water? ▼
Alkaline ionized water is created through electrolysis, which separates water into alkaline and acidic streams. The alkaline water has a higher pH (7.5–9.5), negative ORP (antioxidant value), smaller micro-clustered molecules for better absorption, and activated molecular hydrogen — none of which is present in regular tap or filtered water.
Is Alkonic water safe for children and elderly? ▼
Yes, absolutely. Alkonic water is safe for all age groups including infants (with guidance), children, adults, and senior citizens. In fact, it is particularly beneficial for elderly individuals managing chronic conditions like diabetes, arthritis, or hypertension. Medical-grade ionization ensures no harmful by-products are created.
How does the installation work and how long does it take? ▼
Installation is simple and typically takes 1–2 hours. Our certified technician connects the ionizer to your existing water supply — usually under the kitchen sink or on the countertop. No major plumbing is required. We handle everything and provide a full demonstration before leaving.

What is the DARC auto-cleaning system? ▼
DARC (Dual Automatic Reverse Cleaning) is Alkonic's proprietary plate-maintenance technology. After every use cycle, the system automatically reverses the polarity to remove mineral deposits from the electrolysis plates. This keeps the plates clean without any manual effort, ensuring consistent performance over the 30-year machine life.

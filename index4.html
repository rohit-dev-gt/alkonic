Alkaline water offers superior cellular hydration through micro-clustered molecules, neutralizes excess body acidity, provides antioxidant protection via negative ORP and molecular hydrogen, improves digestion, supports immunity, and many users report improvements in skin, sleep, and conditions like acid reflux within weeks of regular use.
Alkonic ionizers use electrolysis — filtered tap water passes over platinum-coated titanium plates charged with a controlled electrical current. This separates water into alkaline (for drinking) and mildly acidic streams (for external use like cleaning produce). Our 5th-generation technology produces a wider, more consistent ORP range than older machines, and the DARC system cleans the chamber automatically after every cycle.
A certified Alkonic technician visits your home and connects the ionizer directly to your existing kitchen tap. No plumbing modifications are required. Installation typically takes under 2 hours. Our technician will walk you through all pH settings, water modes, and maintenance indicators. Installation is included in your purchase, and we cover all major cities and towns across India.
Alkonic machines are engineered for a 30-year operational lifespan. Platinum-coated titanium plates do not degrade over time with normal use, and our DARC cleaning system prevents the mineral buildup that causes premature failure in competitor products. Every unit comes with a 5-year comprehensive warranty covering parts and on-site labour.
